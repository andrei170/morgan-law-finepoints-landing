FAQ

What bookkeepers ask before they take the quiz.

  • Do I need to already have bookkeeping clients to take the quiz?

    No. The quiz works whether you are completely new or already have a few clients but not where you want to be. One of the five questions is specifically about where you are in the client-load stage. Your answer routes your recommendation accordingly.

  • What is the difference between the Business Coach and the Marketing Coach?

    The Bookkeeper Business Coach covers the structure side: pricing, service packages, processes, the back-end of running a bookkeeping practice. The Bookkeeper Marketing Coach covers the client-getting side: how to find clients, how to position yourself, how to market consistently. Most bookkeepers need both eventually. The quiz tells you which one to do first based on where your biggest constraint is right now.

  • How long does the quiz take?

    Two minutes. Five questions. You enter your email at the result screen and the recommendation arrives immediately.

  • I have already bought one of the courses. Is the quiz still useful?

    Yes. If you have the Business Coach but have not started landing clients consistently, the quiz may point you toward the Marketing Coach as the next step. If you have the Marketing Coach and still feel uncertain about the business structure, it may point you the other way. The quiz looks at where you are right now, not what you have already purchased.

  • Is this quiz made by Fine Points Bookkeeping?

    The quiz logic and funnel page were built by AG1 Consulting Ltd as a sample install for Morgan Law's review. The courses it recommends -- Bookkeeper Business Coach and Bookkeeper Marketing Coach -- are Morgan's own courses on Newzenler. The recommendation engine is the plumbing between what Morgan has already built and the student who is trying to figure out where to start.
